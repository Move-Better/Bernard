What changed here

  • Two subsections inside the bundle card. Today there's one flat “Connect accounts” button + account list. Now: Brand accounts (Instagram & Facebook, one portal on the workspace team) and Google Business — by location (one portal + team per location).
  • Per-location connect. Each location row has its own connect/Manage/Reconnect button → opens a Google-only bundle portal scoped to that location’s team. Reflects bundle’s hard limit of one active GBP per team.
  • Status is per row. Connected (success/emerald), “not connected yet” (Connect button), expired (amber Reconnect — the act-now --action token).
  • Posting fan-out. A Google post now fans out across every connected location, mirroring the existing Buffer GBP fan-out.
  • Locations come from workspace_locations — managed under Settings → Workspace → Locations; this card only connects them.
