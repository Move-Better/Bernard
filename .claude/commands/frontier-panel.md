Run the **Frontier Product Panel** defined in `.claude/frontier-panel.md`. That
file is the living instrument and the source of truth — read it in full first,
then execute the run exactly as §0 describes.

The question this answers: **"what is the most ambitious version of Bernard, and
what would the best people and the best technology in the world do to get there?"**
This is the *unbounded* sibling of `/product-panel` — use it to raise the ceiling,
not optimize the floor.

## The prime stipulation (non-negotiable for this panel)

**Time and cost are NOT factors.** Neither the user's time nor Claude's output
budget may rank, soften, trim, or reject any idea. Optimize for **improvement and
ambition only** — never for saving time or money. A month-long, token-heavy move
that makes Bernard meaningfully better belongs at the TOP of the list. Do NOT use
the Est. Days / Est. Claude Cost format here — that's the pragmatic panel's job.

## Run procedure (follow the doc; this is the checklist)

1. **Ground in the LIVE app first — then go unbounded.** Dispatch an `Explore`
   agent (very thorough) over `src/` + `api/` to read the *real* components,
   routes, and surfaces. Anchor every idea to a real file/route, THEN propose the
   boldest possible evolution of it. Ground first so the ambition has teeth — never
   blue-sky from memory.

2. **Look OUTWARD — required.** Every expert must bring in concrete outside
   references: features from other products worth stealing/surpassing, and
   capabilities from other AI/LLM systems and modalities (realtime voice, native
   video/image gen, long-context agents, computer-use, multi-model routing,
   on-device). Name them specifically.

3. **Convene the seven experts** (keep the cast stable so runs compare): 🎨 Product
   designer (IA) · 🧠 Behavioral scientist · 🎙️ Conversation/voice UX · 🤖 AI/ML
   engineer · 🔧 Reliability engineer · 🔁 Workflow/ops designer · 🛰️ **Frontier
   scout** (the 7th seat — whose whole job is the outward scan: competitors, other
   products, other LLMs to incorporate or leapfrog). Each gives their sharpest read
   + their boldest move + at least one concrete outside reference.

4. **Converge** on the single most ambitious through-line the panel agrees on.

5. **Re-rank the ambition register** — carry forward open IDs, add new bold moves,
   rank **boldest / highest-upside first** (Upside scale: 🚀 transformative · ⬆️
   strong step-change · ✨ meaningful). Status: Open/Aware/Building/Closed. **No cost
   column, no severity, no feasibility ranking.**

6. **Name the frontier bet** — the one move that most changes what Bernard *is*.
   You may sketch a sequence, but never drop a bold idea to make the list
   "shippable."

7. **Append, never overwrite** — add a new dated `## ⟳ Run — <YYYY-MM-DD>` block in
   §2 + an ambition-register update (§3) + a drift-log row (§4: is the ceiling
   rising? did the last frontier release open a new move?).

One pass per invocation — do NOT self-iterate. Always carry the honest caveat:
expert *simulation*, not real-staffer *observation* — weight findings "very likely
the right direction," not "proven."
