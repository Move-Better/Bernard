# Bernard Marketing — ICP + 10 Interview Topics

_Created 2026-06-04. Quick-research brief to drive marketing-content interviews recorded INTO Bernard so Q's clinical voice IS the marketing._

---

## ICP (quick read)

**Primary**: Solo or small-group **hands-on + integrative clinicians** — chiros, PTs, OTs, naturopaths, massage therapists, acupuncturists — who:

- Own their practice (or lead the clinical brand inside one)
- Already believe in content/education as a referral engine but **hate writing**
- Have a strong clinical voice in-person that doesn't translate to their blog/socials
- Are 1–3 person marketing operations (often the clinician + a part-time VA)
- Spend $0–500/mo on marketing tools today; allergic to agencies after one bad experience

**Disqualifiers**:
- Hospital-employed clinicians (no autonomy over content)
- Surgeons (different buyer + voice + economics)
- Pure-aesthetic med spas (different content economy)

**Top pains**:
1. "I know what to say, I just can't sit down and write it"
2. Ghostwriters / agencies don't sound like them
3. ChatGPT output is generic and clinically shallow
4. Social inconsistency → referral pipeline droughts
5. Patient education materials are stale or copy-pasted from textbooks

**Trigger events** (when they start shopping for a Bernard-shaped solution):
- New associate joining the practice
- Opening a 2nd location
- Post-insurance-drop cash-pay pivot
- Podcast launch attempt that stalled
- Hiring a VA they don't know how to direct

## 10 Interview Topics

Each is framed as a topic *Q* (chiropractor, Move Better founder) would speak to into Bernard. The recording becomes the marketing asset — voice fidelity IS the value prop.

| # | Topic | Marketing asset it produces |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Why I stopped paying ghostwriters for my blog** | Founder-story long-form post + LinkedIn carousel |
| 2 | **The 10-minute patient explanation I give every week that should be a blog post** (pick one — e.g., "why your low back hurts when you sit") | Evergreen SEO pillar + email + Reels script |
| 3 | **What a hands-on clinician's content has to do that a hospital marketing team's content doesn't** | Positioning manifesto for Bernard landing page |
| 4 | **The referral conversation I wish every patient could overhear** | Trust-building case-study post + GBP update |
| 5 | **What I learned trying to use ChatGPT for clinic content** (and where it broke) | Comparison post / objection-handler for Bernard site |
| 6 | **A patient story I tell at dinner parties that I've never written down** | Narrative case study (anonymized) — shows voice fidelity |
| 7 | **The three things integrative providers get wrong about social media** | Listicle + Reels series — pulls in chiro/PT/ND audience |
| 8 | **Why I think clinicians, not agencies, should own their voice** | Manifesto post for Bernard ICP + email sequence opener |
| 9 | **The clinic operations changes I made when I stopped writing content myself** | Workflow / behind-the-scenes post — proof of dogfooding |
| 10 | **What I'd tell a clinician opening their second location about marketing** | High-intent post targeting trigger-event #2 (2nd-location opens) |

### Recommended recording order

- **First** — #1, #3, #8 → establish the Bernard point of view
- **Then** — #2, #4, #6 → most reusable clinical content (also good Move Better case study fodder)
- **Last** — #5, #7, #9, #10 → conversion-stage assets for later in the funnel

### Settings to use per interview

- Length: **Expansive** where transcript supports it
- Output: multi-part series (blog + email + social batch) off each recording
- Reuse the SAME transcript across channels — that's the whole point of the platform
