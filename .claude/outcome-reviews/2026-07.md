**1. The video pipeline is a funnel to nowhere: 98 uploads → 97 segments → 0 renders (30d).**
Evidence: §a; all-time 172→3→1; zero `instagram_reel` rows ever. Mechanism: render requires a human to open each clip and export (`clipsLib.js:66` is the only caller of `/api/editorial/render-segments`), and that manual path 504'd for the one staffer who tried (07-13 feedback). Fix already decided: **T2 auto-draft Reels** (in flight per strategy). This review's job next month is to check the number moved — the kill criterion clock is running.

**2. Cadence delivery is 30% of target, and the misses are silent.** 
Evidence: §b/§c — FB token dead ~2 weeks with zero alerting (Meta 190:460), FB 23%, GBP 23%, IG 41%, blog silent 35 days, email/youtube enabled but never planned. Fix already decided: **T1** (day-0 connection-death alert + verified-live links) — #2213/#2214 merged today are the first pieces. Remaining actionable now: GBP's gap is approval throughput (8 created / 2 published), which the approval-stays-heavy decision owns; and `enabled_outputs` lists channels (email, youtube, blog-cadence) the planner never plans — either plan them or disable them so the settings stop overpromising.

**3. The Instagram Story lane is fiction: target 5/wk, published 0 ever, and the planner gave up on 07-01.**
Evidence: §b/§c — 6 drafts all-time, all still `draft`, 4 of 6 have **zero media** (unpublishable as stories), none scheduled, newest 07-01. The publish path is NOT the blocker — `bundlePublisher.js` supports `type: 'STORY'`. The lane fails upstream: drafts are created media-less, never surfaced anywhere staff act on them, and cadence math counts a 5/wk target it can never hit (dragging the whole delivery % down and mis-training any cadence learning). Needs a decision: wire the story lane end-to-end (media required at draft time + a slot in /week) or zero the target until T3 slots exist. Chip spawned.
