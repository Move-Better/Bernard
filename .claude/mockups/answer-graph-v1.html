Is sciatica worse in the morning?

Voice-verified — Dr. Q's actual words (9.2/10) Current as of Jun 28, 2026 — reviewed after Dr. Q's latest interview

Dr. Q's answer:

"Usually, yes — and it's not because you slept wrong. Overnight your discs actually rehydrate and swell a little, so the first hour of the day is when there's the most pressure on the nerve. That's why the first ten steps out of bed feel like the worst ten steps of your day."

"What I tell patients: don't stretch first thing. Walk first — five easy minutes, let the disc settle, then work on mobility. Most people do it in the opposite order and wonder why mornings never improve."

This answer was updated. Dr. Q's earlier guidance (March 2026) suggested stretching on waking; his current approach is walk-then-stretch. The change was confirmed in his June interview — this page updated automatically.
