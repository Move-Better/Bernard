Putting the Move Better logo on social posts

The rule we're proposing: brand through consistency, stamp only what travels. The account avatar already carries the logo on every post. We add a mark only where content leaves your profile — carousels, video, quote cards — so a screenshot or repost still points back to Move Better. Candid photos stay organic (a stamp reads as an ad and loses reach). All marks below are your real Brand Kit assets on real Move Better photos. Toggle the controls to dial in the look.

3 · Editorial / quote card

Default: logo in the composition

A designed graphic (not a photo), so a logo belongs in the layout — anchored in the footer. This is the most-shared format, and the logo is part of what makes it look finished.

Why the logo works here but not on a candid photo: a quote card is a designed artifact — people expect a maker's mark on it, the way a poster has one. A candid photo is documentary — a stamp makes it look like an ad. Same brand, different job for the logo.

4 · The policy Bernard would apply automatically

Set once in Brand Kit, applied by the compositor at publish — identical whether you hit publish or the automated pipeline does. No per-post decision required. These are the proposed defaults:

What we'd build (v1): a placement policy in Brand Kit (defaults above) + the compositor drawing the mark on carousel-last-slide and editorial cards, reading your existing logo_on_dark / logo_on_light / mark_only roles. Deferred: video end-card (a heavier render change). Corner-bug-on-photo is off by default per the "consistency, not stamps" rule — this mockup just proves it's available when you want it.
