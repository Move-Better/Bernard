Two template families · on a layer model · structure only (you tune the designs)

Dashed amber = the principle

The picker has two groups — Photo templates (full-bleed photo + overlay; the photo is the base layer and a look never resizes it) and Text cards (no photo, branded — deliberate, like @imovehp). Templates are editable presets on a layer stack, not locked designs. Click templates in both groups and watch the canvas + the Layers panel change. Placeholder looks in Bernard colours — you'll tune the actual designs.

Templates are editable presets — open any one to change its text styling, colours, and graphic elements. (You'll tune these.)

What's the structure? Every slide is a layer stack. A Photo template = a full-bleed Photo base layer + text/graphic overlay layers (photo is framed with zoom/reposition; the look never touches it). A Text card = no Photo layer, a brand background + text/graphic layers. Switching template only swaps the overlay layers — your photo + its framing stay put.
