PR 2

Timeline precision — zoom · snap · frame-step

Today the timeline has no zoom, no snapping, and no frame-step — hard to cut precisely. Proposed: scroll/pinch to zoom the timeline, trim handles snap to the playhead, and ±1-frame buttons by the transport.

TIMELINE
snap ⭯ playhead
What changes
  • Scroll-wheel / pinch zooms the timeline around the cursor; trim handles snap to the playhead and clip edges within a few px.
  • Frame-step buttons + ←/→ nudge the playhead exactly one frame. Pure interaction — no new visual design, so low-ambiguity.
