Low back pain for movebetter.co/answers

Why does my lower back hurt on one side only?

One-sided low back pain usually isn't about the spot that hurts — it's about how you load that side. Two people with the same scan can have completely different reasons. The only way to know yours is to watch you move.

Why one side and not the other

Your body finds the path of least resistance. If one hip does more of the work when you stand, sit, or lift, that side takes the wear — and that's individual to how you're built and what you do all day.

Is sciatica always caused by a herniated disc?

What changed here & why

  • 1A voice check rides with every answer. Scored at draft time by the same Haiku judge the content pieces use — faithfulness to your captured thinking, sounds-like-you, and a non-diagnostic-safety dimension specific to public medical content.
  • 2Quiet when it passes, loud when it doesn't (the severity-ramp rule). A pass is a small emerald chip in the header — reassurance, not noise. A hold is a solid banner with the exact drift and the failing dimensions.
  • 3Below 7.5, Approve is genuinely blocked — not just a warning you can click past. A low-fidelity answer with your name on it can't reach the public site. The server re-scores the live text on approve, so an inline edit can't sneak a stale pass through.
  • 4The fix path is the one you already have. Edit inline (re-scores on save) or Ask Bernard to revise (re-drafts in your voice + re-scores). No new screen. When it clears 7.5, Approve lights up.
The gate, end to end: Bernard drafts → scores → if low, tries one tighter re-draft automatically → persists the score. You open the queue: pass → approve → (Phase 2) publishes live.
