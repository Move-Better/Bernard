Under the hood

01
Enqueue
POST /api/producer/request drops one row in agent_inbox, scoped to your workspace.
02
Claim
The 5-min agent-tick claims it, inside the same daily draft budget as every other lane.
03
Ground & draft
Finds the best-fit interview, runs draftAtom, writes a status=draft piece.
04
You decide
It waits on /week. You edit, approve, or bin it. Bernard never publishes on his own.
No new database table. The agent_inbox queue already accepts any kind — no migration needed.
Draft-on-topic only. This is not a general chat/intent router — it handles exactly one thing and routes to the existing draft path.
Tenant-scoped & capped. Workspace-filtered like an authz check; a flood of requests can’t blow the daily cap.
