What Bernard is, and how it works

Bernard turns a short conversation with a clinician into a week of on-brand, on-voice content — and helps schedule and publish it everywhere your clinic shows up online. This page explains what it can do today, how the pieces fit together, what just shipped, and what's next.

1 The short version

If you read nothing else, read this.

The big idea

Clinics are full of expertise but short on time. The hard part of marketing isn't posting — it's producing something true and in your own voice, consistently. Bernard fixes the producing: a clinician talks for a few minutes, and Bernard does the writing, the design, the scheduling, and the publishing — sounding like the clinic, not like a robot.

Capture once

One conversation or video becomes many pieces across many channels.

Sounds like you

Bernard learns each clinician's real voice and writes in it — no generic AI filler.

Stays on a rhythm

Content is metered out week by week and published on a sensible schedule automatically.

2 How it works — the pipeline in detail

From a clinician's words to a published post, here's the whole path.

Everything in Bernard rides on one idea: capture the clinician's real expertise once, then let the software do the production work many times over. Nothing is invented out of thin air — every post traces back to something a clinician actually said or showed.

1 · Capture

Content starts as raw material: a guided on-camera interview, a live or phone interview, a quick voice memo, an uploaded handout, or even a web article you want to riff on. There's also a phone-friendly capture page so a clinician can record from any device. All of it is stored safely and transcribed.

2 · Understanding & memory

When an interview finishes, Bernard summarises it, learns from it, and adds it to the clinic's growing "memory" — the accumulated knowledge and phrasing of your team. This memory is what lets later drafts sound consistent and informed rather than starting from zero each time.

3 · Mining & drafting

From that raw material Bernard produces the building blocks of a content plan — a caption here, a blog idea there, a reel clip from the video. For video specifically, Moment Miner scans the footage and ranks the most quotable moments so you're not scrubbing through an hour of tape. Writing happens in the clinician's voice, grounded in the transcript and the clinic's memory.

A deliberate drip, not a dump. A single interview can seed a whole backlog of ideas, but Bernard doesn't fire them all out at once. It meters them into each week up to a sensible per-channel pace, so your feeds stay steady instead of flooding and going quiet.

4 · Plan & approve — "Your Week"

The composed plan lands on the Your Week screen: a clean list of what's slated for the coming week, by channel and day. A producer reviews each piece, edits if needed, and approves & schedules it. Drafts are generated on demand when you open them, so the system isn't burning effort on things you'll never use.

5 · Publish

Approved, scheduled content publishes itself at the right time — to social platforms, Google Business Profile, your blog, and email. Where Bernard can't post directly, it hands you a clean, ready-to-paste export. Once posts are live, their performance flows back into analytics.

4 Moment Miner — video into reels

The standout feature for clinics sitting on video they never use.

Point Bernard at an interview video and it watches the whole thing for you, surfacing the strongest moments — a sharp coaching cue, a breakthrough, a great hook — and rates each on how quotable it is. You skim a ranked feed instead of scrubbing footage, approve the ones you like, and they become polished, captioned reels.

Ranked "moment" feed Live

Best moments first, with a quotability rating, so the good stuff finds you.

Built-in reel editor Live

Reframe, colour-grade to your brand, add captions (pop/fade), titles & lower-thirds, subtle motion, and switch between Reel / Square / Portrait formats.

On purpose: no AI-generated video. Bernard never fabricates footage or synthetic talking heads. Every clip is your real clinician saying real things. This is a deliberate quality and trust choice — it's what keeps the content credible rather than "AI slop."

10 Voice & brand identity

The thing that makes Bernard's output yours, not generic.

Bernard's whole premise is "voice-faithful." It learns the way each clinician actually talks — their characteristic phrases and tone — and writes in it. A behind-the-scenes scorer checks drafts for voice fidelity so output doesn't drift into bland, clinical, or off-brand territory.

  • Per-clinician voice profiles — phrases and tone learned from real interviews. Live
  • Voice-fidelity scoring — an automatic check that drafts still sound like the person. Live
  • Brand Kit — logo, colours, fonts and tone in one place, applied everywhere. Live
  • Spoken voice clone — a clinician's voice for filling gaps in audio; an on-brand, opt-in capability. In progress
A privacy line Bernard won't cross. No patient stories or identifiable case details are used. Content stays at the level of education and "quick wins," never individual outcomes — by design.

11 For tenants — getting set up

How a new clinic joins, and how each workspace stays separate.

Bernard is multi-tenant: one platform serves many clinics, each at its own web address (yourclinic.withbernard.ai). A new clinic self-onboards through a short wizard — business basics (with an optional website scan to pre-fill your voice and brand), pick a web address, choose which channels you want, set up capture, and you're in. Your subdomain works immediately.

Self-serve onboarding Live

A guided wizard creates your workspace, brand kit and first clinician in minutes.

Your own settings Live

Display name, voice, interview style, channels, locations, publish accounts, brand, campaigns and team — all editable.

Strict data separation Live

Each clinic's content, media and connected accounts are walled off from every other clinic.

Roles & access Live

Clinician / producer / admin roles control who can capture, edit, approve, publish and configure.

Under the hood (for the curious): Bernard runs as a single secure deployment. Sign-in and teams are managed through Clerk; media lives in dedicated cloud storage; the app is monitored with error tracking, uptime heartbeats and automatic backups. Every request is scoped to its own clinic so data can't leak across tenants.
