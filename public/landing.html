How it works

Three steps. Most of it isn't yours.

You do the first step. NarrateRx does the messy middle. You get the last word before anything goes out.

1

You talk for 15 minutes

Answer a few friendly questions out loud — about your work, a patient story, something you wish people understood. No writing, no blank page. Just talk like you would to a colleague.

2

It writes a month of content

NarrateRx turns that one conversation into a blog post, social captions, a newsletter, and a Google update — all from your own words, in a voice that sounds like you instead of a robot.

3

You review and publish

Skim the drafts between patients, tweak anything you want, and post straight to your blog, social, and Google with a click. Nothing goes out until you say so.

Why it's different

Other tools start from a blank box. This one starts from you.

Most AI writers wait for you to type a prompt and hand back something generic. NarrateRx starts from the one thing only you have — your voice and your ideas.

It sounds like you, not a robot

Every post comes from what you actually said in your interview. The more you use it, the more it picks up your phrasing — so it reads like you wrote it, not like AI filler.

Built for clinics, not generic marketing

NarrateRx understands hands-on and integrative care — chiropractic, equine, animal, coaching. It knows your audience and your topics, so you're not fighting a tool built for software ads.

Posts straight to where you publish

Blog, social, Google Business, and email — sent right where they need to go, or exported clean for whatever you already use. No copy-paste tour, no learning a new system.
