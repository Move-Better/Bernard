How it works

Three steps. Most of it isn't yours.

You do step one. Bernard does the hard part. You have the last word before anything goes out.

1

Bernard asks. You answer.

Pick a question — a recent patient win, something you explain every week, a perspective your audience needs to hear. Answer it out loud for up to 90 seconds. No writing. No blank page.

2

Your words become posts.

Bernard turns your answer into a blog post, social captions, a newsletter, and a Google Business update — all written from what you actually said, never from a generic AI prompt.

3

You review and publish.

Skim the drafts between patients, tweak anything you want, and publish to WordPress, Buffer (Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, Google Business), and Beehiiv with one click. Nothing goes out until you say so.

Why Bernard is different

Other tools start from a blank box. Bernard starts from you.

Most AI writing tools wait for you to type a prompt. Bernard asks the questions himself — so every word in every post comes from what you actually know and said.

Every word traces back to yours

Nothing is invented. Every claim, story, and phrase in a Bernard post comes from your interview answers. You can trace any sentence back to what you said — and so can your patients.

It sounds like you, not AI

Bernard listens for how you talk: your phrasing, your examples, the way you explain hard things simply. The more you use it, the more the posts sound like something you actually wrote.

Posts straight to where they need to go

WordPress, Buffer (Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, and 6 more channels), Beehiiv, and Google Business Profile — published right from the app, or exported clean for whatever you already use. No copy-paste tour. No second tool.
